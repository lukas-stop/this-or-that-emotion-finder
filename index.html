This is a tool created to help those with alexithymia to have a less overwhelming emotion wheel!

How to Use:

To use this app, begin by selecting one of the 7 core emotions. Next, pick whether your original selection still fits strongly, or the next relevant emotion fits your emotions better. If you want to change your mind, press the "go back" button and reselect the more fitting option. Once you've run out of emotions, your final selection will be displayed to you!
